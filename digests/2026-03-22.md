- The authors propose a measurement-induced quantum neural network (MINN) architecture that incorporates mid-circuit measurement outcomes to determine subsequent entangling gates.

**Results:**

- The authors note that a generic MINN is not expected to be efficiently classically simulable, which has implications for the scalability of the approach.
